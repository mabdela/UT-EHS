Safety Checklist General Safety
  • Bench tops and work areas free of unsafe clutter
  • Waste safely stored and labelled
  • Fume hood is uncluttered
  • Lab coats, long pants and closed shoes are worn
  • Sash closed when hood not in use, and as low as practical when used
  • Trained in WHMIS and GHS
  • Buddy system used for work alone
